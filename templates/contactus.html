Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about StreetEats Connect

How do I register as a vendor on StreetEats Connect?

Registration is simple! Click on 'Sign Up' button, choose 'Vendor' option, fill in your basic details, business information, and upload required documents like FSSAI license. Our team will verify your application within 24-48 hours.

What are the benefits of group buying?

Group buying allows vendors to combine orders and purchase in bulk, resulting in 15-30% cost savings. You also get better quality assurance, shared delivery costs, and access to premium suppliers who typically only deal with large orders.

How does the PM SVANidhi loan integration work?

We've simplified the PM SVANidhi application process. Through our platform, you can apply for collateral-free loans up to â‚¹10,000 with guided assistance, document verification support, and digital repayment options. The entire process is streamlined within our app.

What quality assurance measures do you have?

We implement digital quality checks with supplier photo/video uploads, maintain a robust vendor feedback system, provide simple rapid testing kits for vendors, and have community-driven quality control. We're also implementing blockchain for end-to-end traceability.

How does the delivery system work?

Our integrated logistics system offers scheduled deliveries on your preferred days and times, temperature-controlled handling for perishables, direct delivery to your stall location, and quality guarantee with inspection rights upon delivery.

What are the platform fees?

Our platform charges a minimal service fee of 2-3% on transactions, which is much lower than traditional intermediaries. We also offer volume-based discounts for regular users and group orders. Registration and basic platform access are completely free.

Can I become a supplier on your platform?

Yes! We welcome verified suppliers with proper licenses and certifications. You'll need FSSAI certification, GST registration, and other relevant permits. Contact us through the 'Supplier Partnership' option above for detailed onboarding information.

Is there customer support available?

Absolutely! We provide 24/7 customer support through phone, WhatsApp, email, and in-app chat. Our multilingual support team can assist you in Hindi, English, and other regional languages. We typically respond within 2 hours during business hours.
